02 The second brain what the integrations add up to

It learns. It maintains.
It remembers.

Connected across your whole stack, Vibe11 becomes the institutional memory of your engineering organization — the credibility layer that proves your product is professionally maintained.

▸ Learn

Every incident, dependency quirk, flaky test, and architecture decision is indexed into a living model of your system — pulled from your repos, tickets, dashboards, and docs.

▸ Maintain

Agents act on that knowledge end to end: a Datadog alert becomes a diagnosed root cause, a fix PR in GitHub, a closed Jira ticket, and a Slack summary — before you've read the alert.

▸ Remember

Every action is documented back into Confluence or Notion with a full audit trail. Knowledge that used to live in one founder's head becomes an asset the whole org — and your investors — can see.
